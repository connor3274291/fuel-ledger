1. What we collect

  • Account info: the email and password you sign up with (your password itself is never stored by us — our backend provider, Supabase, stores it hashed and encrypted).
  • Profile info: the username you choose, which is visible to other users on the leaderboard.
  • Nutrition data: the food, water, supplement, and goal entries you log, which are private to your account and not visible to other users.
  • Leaderboard scores: a daily score derived from your goals vs. your logged intake, visible alongside your username to other users.

2. What we don't collect

We don't collect your real name, location, or any health/medical information beyond what you voluntarily type into your own food log. We don't sell your data to advertisers.

3. How your data is stored

Data is stored with Supabase, a third-party database provider, under access rules ("row-level security") that restrict your private logs and goals to your account only. The public leaderboard table is readable by any signed-in user by design.

4. How we use it

Solely to run the App: to show you your own logs and goals, and to calculate and display your leaderboard score to other users. We don't use your data to train AI models or share it with advertisers.

5. Your choices

  • You can edit or delete any logged entry at any time.
  • You can request full account deletion, including your leaderboard history, by emailing [support email].
  • You can choose a username that isn't your real name if you don't want your identity tied to your leaderboard score.
